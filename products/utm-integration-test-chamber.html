UTM Integration Test Chamber

UTM Integration Test Chambers are designed to operate with Universal Testing Machines for mechanical testing under controlled environmental conditions. They support tensile, compression, and fatigue testing at elevated or sub-zero temperatures and are suitable for material characterization and product validation.

UTM Integration Test Chamber

UTM Integration Test Chambers are designed to operate with Universal Testing Machines for mechanical testing under controlled environmental conditions. They support tensile, compression, and fatigue testing at elevated or sub-zero temperatures and are suitable for material characterization and product validation.
