Thermal Shock Series

Thermal Shock Chambers are designed to expose test specimens to rapid and extreme temperature changes using dual- or multi-zone configurations. These systems evaluate material integrity, thermal fatigue, and failure modes caused by sudden thermal stress. They are widely used in electronics, aerospace, and automotive qualification testing. Custom chamber sizes are available on request.
