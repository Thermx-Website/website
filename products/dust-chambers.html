Dust Chambers

XD Series chambers are intended for dust-ingress IP5X and IP6X testing in accordance with IEC and ISO standards. They feature CRCA powder-coated outer panels and SS 304 inner construction, with uniform dust circulation and controlled air extraction through a centrifugal blower, vacuum system, and dust-recovery mechanism.
