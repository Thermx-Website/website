Our Product Range

Advanced Systems for Environmental Testing

Precision-engineered chambers for thermal, climatic, ingress, vibration, mechanical, battery, and corrosion validation.

01 — ENV

Environmental Test Chambers

Environmental Test Chambers are engineered to accurately simulate extreme temperature and humidity conditions, enabling precise evaluation of product performance, reliability, and durability.

02 — TSC

Thermal Shock Series

Thermal Shock Chambers expose specimens to rapid and extreme temperature changes using dual or multi-zone configurations.

03 — ALT

Altitude Series

Altitude Test Chambers simulate high-altitude and low-pressure environments for aerospace, defence, and aviation testing.

04 — BAT

Battery Test Chamber

Safe testing of lithium-ion and other energy storage systems under controlled temperature and humidity conditions.

05 — XR

Rain Chambers

Water-ingress testing from IPX1 to IPX9K with robust SS 304 construction and precision spray systems.

06 — XD

Dust Chambers

Dust-ingress testing for IP5X and IP6X with uniform circulation, vacuum extraction, and dust recovery.

07 — VIB

Vibration Shaker Integration Chamber

Combined environmental and mechanical vibration testing for real-life operational stress simulation.

08 — UTM

UTM Integration Test Chamber

Controlled environmental testing integrated with Universal Testing Machines for tensile, compression, and fatigue tests.

09 — COR

Corrosion Test Chambers

Accelerated salt spray and cyclic corrosion exposure for materials and coating resistance evaluation.
