Recommended settings:

- 16:9 landscape
- 1280 x 720 or 1920 x 1080
- Muted/no audio
- 3 to 10 seconds
- Keep the same filename so no code changes are required.

The video automatically plays when the Coming Soon card is hovered or focused, and resets when the pointer leaves.
